Blockchain Technology Explained

Blockchain is a decentralized and distributed ledger technology that provides transparency and security. Here are some essential aspects:

  • Each block in the chain contains data and a reference to the previous block.
  • Data is stored on a network of nodes, making it tamper-resistant.
  • Transactions are verified through consensus mechanisms like Proof of Work or Proof of Stake.
  • Blockchain has applications beyond cryptocurrency, including supply chain management, healthcare, and more.
